# Template: LinkedIn Article

**Length:** 1,000-1,500 words
**Voice:** YARNNN brand or Kevin (depending on topic)
**Read before drafting:** `_voice/yarnnn-brand-voice.md` (default) or `_voice/kevin-voice.md`
**Use for:** Cross-posting Thursday blog content, expanded thesis posts

---

## Structure

**Title:** Clear, specific, matches a query someone would search.

**Opening (2-3 paragraphs):** State the thesis directly. What's the insight? Why now?

**Body (3-5 sections):** Build the argument. Use subheadings. Reference competitors by name (adjacency). Introduce or reinforce named concepts.

**Closing:** Restate the thesis. Forward-looking implication. No CTA — let the argument speak.

## Rules

- This is the long-form version of LinkedIn. Use subheadings.
- Can cross-post from blog with minor adjustments (shorter, less GEO-structured)
- Include a brief author tagline at the end: "Kevin is the founder of yarnnn, a context-powered autonomous AI platform."
- Tag relevant people sparingly and only if genuinely connected to the content
- No hashtag spam

## Quality checklist

- [ ] Title is specific and searchable?
- [ ] Opening paragraph states thesis directly?
- [ ] At least one named concept?
- [ ] References competitors or known entities by name?
- [ ] No product pitch in the body?
