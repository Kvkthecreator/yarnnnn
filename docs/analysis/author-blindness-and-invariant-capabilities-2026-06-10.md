## 1. The author-blindness filter (the operator's insight — hold this one)

> **An act is author-blind when its value to the consequence-bearer is invariant
> to who — or what — performed it. An act is author-bound when its value derives
> from the author's personhood.**

The insight arrived as the newsletter objection: paid newsletters monetize the
*person*. The subscriber buys authored personhood. An AI-produced newsletter
doesn't merely fail to capture that value — **it destroys it on discovery.** The
workflow can be delegated; the authorship cannot. "Voice continuity" marketed as
delegation is, in that market, the exact thing the audience punishes.

### 1a. It's a spectrum, not a binary

| Act | Author-blindness | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Submit a trade | **Fully blind** | P&L does not care who clicked. Money-truth is the most author-blind signal that exists — this is *why* trading always felt like the clean case. |
| Internal decision-support artifact (signal eval, research memo, deal brief, risk analysis) | **Mostly blind** | Value is correctness and timeliness. Consumed by the operator or their org, not an audience buying a person. |
| Client deliverable under the operator's name | **Semi-bound** | Correctness dominates, but the relationship carries authorship expectations. Delegation is acceptable if quality holds and disclosure norms are met. |
| Audience-facing personal authorship (paid newsletter, byline essay, personal brand content) | **Author-bound** | The person IS the product. Delegation of authorship is value-destroying, not value-capturing. |

### 1b. The ICP filter, updated (now four qualifiers)

> Bounded operation **+** repeating consequential decision **+** track record
> they're not learning from **+** **author-blind act.**

The fourth qualifier is the one this doc adds. It must be applied to every
future wedge/persona/bundle discussion — *recorded here precisely so it is not
re-lost to ambiguity or forgetfulness.* The break test for any candidate ICP:
"if the consequence-bearer's audience/counterparty discovered the act was
performed by a delegate, does the value survive?" If no → author-bound → not a
lead ICP for the "produces the work on your behalf" USP.

### 1c. What it does to the act-shape map

The 2026-06-09 act-shape map's **artifact class splits in half**:

- **Decision-support artifacts** (memos, evals, briefs, analyses — consumed to
  decide) → author-blind → **pass.** Still kernel-served, zero integration
  debt. This is the surviving broad center.
- **Audience-facing authored artifacts** (newsletters, essays, personal brand
  content) → author-bound → **fail as a lead.** Retired: the monetized
  newsletter operator as lead ICP; "creator with an audience" as a lead persona.

Nuance held consciously: author-bound operations can still be **served** — as a
research/ops/workflow delegate that produces *inputs* the human authors from.
But that is assistant terrain (commodity, incumbents' home turf) and must never
be *marketed* with the produces-the-work USP. Served-not-led.

### 1d. Why this filter is strategically convenient, not just true

Author-blind acts are exactly where the moat binds hardest: where the act's
value is outcome-determined, ground truth exists, the calibration loop can
close, and the judgment seat matters because being wrong costs something. The
filter doesn't shrink the addressable space arbitrarily — it shrinks it *toward
the differentiator.*

## 2. The general-vs-narrow adjudication (the ambition, handled honestly)

The operator named the real conflict: the ambition is a general-purpose Agent
OS (Cowork-class), and the docs + codebase direction point there; narrow
consumer profiles feel like a retreat. Adjudicated:

- **Against horizontal-first GTM:** at first touch a general agent workspace is
  undifferentiated and competes on claim language already commoditized (June-10
  audit). Cowork can be horizontal because Anthropic has zero CAC + owns model
  and surface. Bare-kernel activation is unproven even internally (proxy
  operators needed full program scaffolding). Every admired general platform
  earned generality late, through a wedge (S3→AWS, snowboards→Shopify,
  Code→Cowork).
- **For keeping the horizontal alive:** the category is young; OpenClaw proved
  horizontal appetite exists unaided; the kernel/program architecture
  (ADR-222) is real platform architecture, already CI-enforced.
- **The resolution — mostly a false binary:** ADR-222 already separates what
  the platform *is* (general) from how it *activates* (a program).

> **Posture (ratified): general in architecture, narrow in motion, horizontal
> as background radiation.** Kernel stays domain-agnostic; activation pushes
> happen one program at a time; the horizontal surface (Layer-1 interop + the
> mechanism essays) runs passively at ~zero marginal cost. If horizontal pull
> appears organically, the funnel will show it without having bet on it.

Grounding observation (recorded because the operator asked for groundedness):
the repo shows enormous kernel investment, 300+ ADRs, two active programs,
world-class eval infra — and zero strangers. The moat audit already named
activation as the binding constraint. **The marginal ADR now adds less than the
marginal stranger would teach.**

## 3. The invariant capability set (the committed build direction)

The capabilities oscillation was downstream of the unresolved ICP question. The
resolution: identify the capabilities **invariant across every path the
operator is torn between** (trader-deepening · any author-blind vertical ·
horizontal OS), build those, and defer everything path-contingent. The
indecision then only gates bundle-side work — small by design.

### Invariant 1 — Ground-truth ingestion as a kernel capability

**Concept:** the differentiator is "judgment calibrated against what actually
happened." That requires *what actually happened* to enter the system. Today
reality enters through exactly **two pipes** — Alpaca and Lemon Squeezy.

**Receipts (audited 2026-06-10):**
- `api/services/outcomes/base.py` — `OutcomeProvider` ABC + `OutcomeCandidate`
  shape (idempotency keys, `proposal_id` FK, signed `outcome_value_cents`,
  `context_domain` routing). The docstring already anticipates the
  generalization: *"Each domain (trading, commerce, email, ...)"*.
- `api/services/outcomes/reconciler.py` — `DEFAULT_PROVIDERS = [Trading,
  Commerce]`. Two. Both require a live platform API.
- `api/services/primitives/mirror_calibration.py` + `bundle_reader.py:250
  get_ground_truth_for_workspace` — the ADR-327 self-improving loop reads the
  program-declared `substrate_abi.ground_truth` file.
- **alpha-author's MANIFEST declares NO `ground_truth`** (grep: zero hits).
  The second active program's calibration loop is structurally dark — the
  Reviewer renders verdicts into a void; `_calibration.md` has nothing to
  mirror. The moat is live for roughly one persona.

**The fill-in:** (a) a **manual/CSV OutcomeProvider** — the universal fallback
that lets any operation (sales, audience, deals, anything) import outcomes as
rows with the existing idempotency + proposal-linking semantics; (b)
ground-truth substrate declarations + accumulation recurrences for non-trading
programs (alpha-author first); (c) per-vertical API connectors later, as cheap
bundle-side adds. **No kernel redesign — the ABC and the MANIFEST slot were
built for exactly this and are simply under-populated.**

**Why invariant:** trader-deepening needs richer reconciliation; any vertical
needs its outcome pipe; the horizontal story needs "bring your own outcome
signal." All three run through this capability. It converts the judgment seat
from a trading feature into a platform property.

### Invariant 2 — History ingestion + retrospective reconciliation

**Concept:** compounding takes tenure; strangers have none — but nearly every
target operator has a *past* (archive, trade log, deal history, decision log).
Ingest the past and reconcile it (what was decided → what happened → what the
pattern says) and the calibration trail exists at **hour one** instead of month
three. This is the cold-start seam (ADR-320) answered as a feature, and it
doubles as substrate seeding every workspace needs anyway.

**Receipts:** uploads are single-file, 25MB, typed (`routes/documents.py`) — no
bulk/archive path. Outcomes enter only via live platform-event providers — so
historical decisions have **no entry point** into the calibration substrate.
The Reviewer + mirror can already consume whatever lands in substrate; the
missing piece is ingestion + a retrospective mode of the reconciler.

**The convergence that makes this cheap:** a CSV/manual OutcomeProvider
(Invariant 1a) **is** most of Invariant 2's machinery — the same pipe, pointed
at the past. Build once, serve both.

### Invariant 3 — Artifact/compose production quality

**Concept:** whatever path wins, the dividends are artifacts, and artifact
quality is the perceived product quality. Provenance and
correction-compounding only impress if the artifact clears the incumbent
baseline. Ranked third because it removes a discount factor rather than
unlocking the differentiator.

**Receipts:** `render/compose.py` dispatches 11 section kinds (narrative,
callout, checklist, metric-cards, entity-grid, comparison-table, status-matrix,
data-table, timeline, trend/distribution charts) — strong for reports; ADR-225
notes library components shipped as "deliberate placeholders — visual polish is
incremental"; deck/visual richness is mid.

### Explicitly path-contingent (deferred without cost)

Per-vertical connectors beyond the CSV fallback · publish/distribution
last-mile (commodity — absorb via headless/integration) · bundle #3 · vertical
marketing assets. These wait for the evidence the invariant build + background
funnel produce.

## 4. The one-arc reading

The three invariants are mostly **one engineering arc**: *generalize the
outcome pipe (1), point it at the past (2), polish the dividend (3).* It is
provider/bundle-layer work over kernel slots that already exist — not a
re-architecture. When it lands: the calibration loop is experiencable for any
operation that can produce a row of outcomes; day-one activation exists for
anyone with a history; and the ICP lock becomes an evidence question (which
author-blind, fast-loop instance shows pull) instead of a positioning debate.

**Carried open, by design:** the ICP lock (now filtered by §1b's four
qualifiers); the layman noun; the pricing ADR; the Path-A proof decision.
