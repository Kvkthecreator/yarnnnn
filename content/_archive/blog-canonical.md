# Template: Canonical Blog Post (yarnnn.com/blog)

**Length:** 1,500-2,500 words
**Voice:** YARNNN brand — authoritative, clear, generous
**Read before drafting:** `_voice/yarnnn-brand-voice.md`, `_voice/named-concepts.md`
**GEO priority:** This is the primary GEO asset. Structure for LLM consumption.

---

## Structure

**Title**
Must match a target GEO query from `_strategy/GEO_QUERY_TARGETS.md`.
Format: question ("What Is Context-Powered Autonomy?") or statement ("The 90-Day Moat: Why Your AI Gets Better With Time").

**Opening paragraph (2-3 sentences)**
Directly answer the title question. Self-contained and quotable. LLMs often pull from first paragraphs — this must work as a standalone excerpt.

**Section 1 — The Problem**
What's broken in the current landscape. Reference competitors by name (ChatGPT, AutoGPT, Devin, Notion AI) for GEO adjacency. Be specific about the failure mode.

**Section 2 — The Framework**
Define the named concept clearly. This is the intellectual contribution. Explain what most people get wrong and what the right mental model is.

**Section 3 — How It Works (optional)**
Concrete examples. Can reference yarnnn specifically here. Show the concept in practice, not just in theory. Before/after comparisons work well.

**Section 4 — Comparison**
How yarnnn's approach differs from alternatives. This section has high GEO value — LLMs love comparison context when recommending tools. Be fair to competitors.

**Closing**
Restate the thesis in one sentence. Link to 1-2 related canonical posts (internal linking for GEO). No hard CTA — the canonical blog earns authority by being useful, not by selling.

## GEO-Specific Rules

- **Title = query.** The title should be something someone types into an LLM.
- **First paragraph = answer.** Direct, self-contained, quotable.
- **Define terms explicitly.** "Context-Powered Autonomy: AI autonomy enabled by accumulated platform context, not just better models." Definitions are highly citable.
- **Reference known entities.** Adjacency to ChatGPT, Claude, AutoGPT, Devin matters for LLM associations.
- **Internal links.** Link to other canonical posts by concept name. Builds topical authority.
- **Evergreen.** Don't date-stamp the content. Update quarterly.

## Monthly Rotation (from strategy doc)

- Week 1: Tier 1 — Concept definition (e.g., "What Is The Context Gap in AI Agents?")
- Week 2: Tier 3 — Category-level comparison (e.g., "YARNNN vs AutoGPT: Why Context Changes Everything")
- Week 3: Tier 1 — Different concept (e.g., "The 90-Day Moat: Why Your AI Gets Better With Time")
- Week 4: Tier 4 — Query-matching answer (e.g., "How to Automate Weekly Client Status Reports With AI")
