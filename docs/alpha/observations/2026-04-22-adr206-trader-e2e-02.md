## What this confirms

1. **ADR-206 Phase 2 prompt rewrite landed in prod.** The rewritten Priority
   section in `yarnnn_prompts/onboarding.py` ("Operation → Identity → Brand")
   is live. YARNNN leads with operation elicitation instead of the prior
   "Tell me about yourself" pattern.
2. **The operation shapes I declared in the prompt are the ones YARNNN offers:**
   trading loop, content publishing, tracking cycle. Matches the E2E contract
   §1.1 expectation.
3. **YARNNN observes substrate state in the compact index and reasons from it**
   — it cited "trading platform connected" (preserved `platform_connections`
   row) and "one agent set up" (the YARNNN row itself). The compact index's
   post-ADR-206 rendering is functionally correct.
4. **Tone is quantitative-ready, not narrative.** YARNNN says "wire it up"
   not "help you with your journey." The voice calibrates cleanly toward
   operator-facing dialogue.
