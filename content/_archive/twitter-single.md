# Template: Twitter Single

**Max:** 280 characters
**Voice:** Kevin (default)
**Read before drafting:** `_voice/kevin-voice.md`

---

## Structure

One idea. Sharp, opinionated, thesis-connected. Should provoke a reaction: agreement, disagreement, or a quote-tweet adding their own take.

## Types

**Hot take:** "AI in 2026: incredibly powerful, completely amnesiac."
**Pain observation:** "I use ChatGPT every day. Every day, it forgets everything."
**Reframe:** "You're not using AI. You're being AI's memory."
**Question:** "What if the bottleneck in AI productivity isn't the model — it's context?"
**Milestone (build-in-public):** "This week: first deliverable that needed zero edits."

## Rules

- One named concept per tweet (max)
- No hashtags
- No links unless essential (kills engagement)
- No "I just published a blog post" tweets (separate format)
- Must connect to the thesis, even loosely — no random commentary

## Quality checklist

- [ ] Under 280 chars?
- [ ] Would get engagement even without the yarnnn context?
- [ ] Not a product ad?
- [ ] Sharp enough to quote-tweet?
