# Brand

> **Operator**: author this if your authored work is brand-attached (newsletter brand, podcast brand, author imprint, screenwriter portfolio). For pure-personal authorship (e.g., screenplay-in-development with no public presence yet), this file may stay sparse — your IDENTITY + voice carry the brand at this stage.

## Voice

> Distinct from `operation/authored/_voice.md` (voice fingerprint at the prose/sentence level). BRAND.md voice is the *brand-level* voice — the public-facing persona attached to your name or imprint. For most authors, brand voice and authorial voice are the same; for some (e.g., authors who run multiple brands or write under multiple names), they diverge.

## Visual

> Logo, color palette, typography preferences. Relevant for audience-bearing workspaces (newsletter design, social header graphics, podcast cover art). Optional for pre-audience workspaces.
