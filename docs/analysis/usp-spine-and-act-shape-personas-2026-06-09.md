## 1. The spine — four beats, not one word

The discourse tried to find a single word for the relationship (judgment? trust?
continuity?) and correctly failed: **they are not synonyms competing for one
slot — they are facets of one relationship at three moments in time, plus the
payoff.** The discipline is to assign each word its job, not to pick a winner.

| Beat | When it operates | What it names | The sentence it owns |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Entrust** | at the start — the handoff | you give it authority over a judgment | *"You hand it the call."* |
| **Judgment** | every cycle — the substance | what it carries is **your** discipline, not generic competence | *"It makes the call the way you would."* |
| **Continuity** | while you're gone — the absence | it holds the thread your attention drops | *"It keeps the operation going and on-record when you're not there."* |
| **Compounding** | over tenure — the payoff | it gets sharper each cycle, doesn't reset | *"It gets better the longer it runs."* |

Read in order they **are** the product: *you entrust it your judgment; it carries
that judgment with continuity; and it compounds.* Trust is the entry emotion,
judgment is the substance, continuity is the mechanism, compounding is the payoff.

The noun that holds all four is **delegate** — but "delegate" is now backed by an
explicit four-beat, not doing the work alone. The guardrail: the word welded to
"delegate" must be **judgment**, never **task**. You delegate *a judgment*, not a
chore. "Hand it your judgment once" keeps it on the moat side of the line;
"an AI that does your tasks" is the commodity it must not collapse into.

> This is the human-facing expression of FOUNDATIONS Axiom 8 + THESIS Commitment 2
> (the judgment seat). The architecture already holds it; this is its translation.

## 2. The USP — distilled

**The frame (one phrase):**
> A **standing delegate** for the operation you can't be present for.

**The USP (corrected per §5 — "produces the work" sits alongside "makes the
calls"; the act is integration-independent for the broadest persona class):**
> A standing delegate that **produces the work — and makes the calls — on your
> behalf, the way you would, and compounds instead of resetting.**

**The one-liner (copy seed, not final copy):**
> *It runs on your behalf. It gets better the longer it does.*

The two clauses are the two load-bearing words the operator isolated:
*on your behalf* = delegation across presence (continuity);
*better the longer it does* = delegation across time (compounding).

## 3. The psychographic — frame, not occupation

Hardened frame (supersedes the occupation-list ICPs in GTM v3.0 + NARRATIVE):

> **Someone with something that's theirs to run, that they care about getting
> right, that they can't be continuously present for — and who refuses to let it
> reset every time they step away.**

Three load-bearing qualifiers:

- **"theirs to run"** — they hold the consequence (Axiom 8: consequence-bearing).
  Filters to the principal, not a delegate-of-a-delegate.
- **"can't be continuously present for"** — non-presence is the entry condition.
  The scenarios that produce non-presence are many (the double-job operator; the
  overloaded operator spread across too many things; the away-from-desk operator
  whose operation runs on a clock that isn't theirs; the future-self who wants
  today's judgment to compound forward). **The frame is the non-presence; the
  scenarios are the reasons for it.** "Second job" is the most vivid scenario,
  not the frame.
- **"refuses to let it reset"** — the felt pain *and* the willingness-to-pay
  signal. Someone who **winces** at the reset will pay to stop it. This is the
  qualifier that separates the customer from the hobbyist (who doesn't care if it
  resets). The felt pain in layman terms: *"I keep starting over, and I can't
  tell if I'm actually getting better at this."*

**Motion-fit (from `positioning…§4a`):** this is a premium, high-WTP,
high-switching-cost psychographic → a **high-ACV, expansion-led** business, not a
volume/self-serve one. Low-volume is the correct shape, not a hedge to escape.
The stale "$19/mo, five domain experts" framing priced it for the wrong motion.

## 4. The persona reality, re-keyed on ACT-SHAPE (the correction)

The positioning doc's earlier persona tiering keyed on **external-write integration
coverage** — which produced the wrong conclusion that most non-trader personas are
"advisory only, can't act." **That was a category error** (diagnosed §5). The
right axis is: **what shape is the consequential act?** On that axis, coverage is
much broader than the integration map suggests.

| Act shape | The consequential act IS… | Built today? | Personas |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Artifact** | **substrate creation** — `WriteFile` + the compose pipeline (text, articles-with-images, slide decks, briefs, memos) | ✅ **kernel, universal, zero integration debt** | author · creator · analyst · researcher · A&R-who-writes-evals · dealmaker-who-produces-memos |
| **Transaction** | external API write (submit order, create/update product, discount) | ✅ where integration exists (Alpaca, Lemon Squeezy) | trader · store-operator |
| **Message** | send (email today; social = commodity headless add — §5) | ✅ email (Resend); ⚠️ social via headless last-mile | newsletter operator · outreach dealmaker |

**The load-bearing correction:** for the **artifact class**, the act *is the
artifact existing* — in your voice, at quality, on cadence. That act is
`WriteFile` + compose, which is **kernel and already built**, so the
"acts on your behalf" half of the USP is **true on day one with zero platform
connections** for the broadest persona class. The artifact class is not "advisory
only"; it is a **full delegate today**, because for it the substrate IS the act.

Receipts (the act surface, verified 2026-06-09):
- **Transaction writes:** `platform_tools.py` — `TRADING_WRITE_TOOLS`
  (`platform_trading_submit_order` / `cancel_order` / `close_position`),
  `COMMERCE_WRITE_TOOLS` (`create_product` / `update_product` / `create_discount`),
  `write_email` (`platform_email_send` / `send_bulk`).
- **Artifact act:** kernel `WriteFile` (`api/services/primitives/workspace.py`) +
  the compose pipeline (`render/compose.py` + section-kind rendering, ADR-177/213)
  — text, structured data, charts, slides, PDF/HTML. No platform connection
  required.
- **Read-only / partial (not act-on-behalf):** Slack + Notion are operator-DM /
  operator-page-comment only (`SYSTEM_INFRASTRUCTURE_TOOLS`, ADR-304 D2); GitHub
  is read-only. These do NOT deliver audience-facing act-on-behalf — correctly
  noted, but they are not the broad-persona blocker.

## 5. Publish is a commodity last-mile — the retired finding + the caveat

The positioning doc's "**highest-leverage buildout = publish-on-behalf**" finding
is **retired.** The reasoning that retires it:

For artifact operators, the **bottleneck was never distribution** — it was the
artifact getting made the way they'd make it, while they weren't there. The
*publish* (push the finished piece to LinkedIn / X / a newsletter platform) is a
**trailing, low-judgment, commoditizing step**: a button, a webhook, a
Buffer/Typefully/Zapier integration, or a thin headless poster. Building it
custom would spend scarce effort on the commodity half. **Absorb it via
headless / integration services — easy, expansive, low-judgment.** It is not the
leverage point.

The real leverage stays where the product already invests: **artifact quality +
voice/continuity + the compounding seat.** If anything, the high-leverage
*production* buildout is **richer artifact output** (better slides, visual /
structured output), not distribution.

**The one caveat to hold consciously** (so "publish is commodity" doesn't
over-generalize): the *publish step* commoditizes, but **the cadence-and-voice
continuity of shipping does not.**

- **Publish (commodity):** "push this finished piece to LinkedIn." → buy/webhook
  it; don't build it.
- **Cadence-holding distribution (NOT commodity):** "ship something every
  Tuesday, in my voice, even when I'm heads-down — and don't let the channel go
  silent." That is **continuity applied to output** — a delegate behavior driven
  by the recurrence + the judgment seat, with the headless poster as the cheap
  last mile.

Discipline: absorb the publish *mechanism* via integration; keep the *distribution
discipline* (cadence + voice continuity of shipping) inside the product. The first
is commodity; the second is the moat applied to output.

## 6. The one open edge — the layman ICP noun (deferred to the GTM regroup)

Everything above is hardened **in shape.** The single remaining gap (carried from
`positioning…§6a`): the ICP is right in shape but **not yet in a layman noun.**

- The *frame* ("standing delegate / runs on your behalf / compounds") is solid.
- The *felt pain* ("I keep starting over; I can't tell if I'm getting better") is
  solid and layman-resonant.
- The *self-identification noun* — what the lead-vertical user says when they
  describe themselves ("I'm a ___" / "I run a ___") — is **not settled.**
  "Operator," "bounded operation," "double job" are all either system-coded or a
  scenario, not the noun.

This is the explicit, scoped job of the **next sequenced step (the full
GTM_POSITIONING v4 + NARRATIVE regroup)**, which should:
1. Work **bottom-up** from how the lead vertical's real people talk — not
   top-down from this doc's architecture vocabulary.
2. **Pressure-test the bounded-operation / non-presence wedge** (break attempt:
   *"isn't this just hobbyists who don't pay?"* — answer to stress:
   consequential-enough-to-pay; the angel, not the hobbyist; the *wince* is the
   filter).
3. Resolve the **layman noun** + whether it generalizes across ≥2 verticals or
   needs per-vertical surface copy over the shared spine.
4. **Then** retire the stale occupation framings in canon (GTM v3.0
   "$19/mo, five domain experts"; NARRATIVE "solo consultants") and re-frame the
   motion toward high-ACV expansion-led (§3).

Treat this doc as the **skeleton the GTM pass dresses**, not ship-ready copy.
